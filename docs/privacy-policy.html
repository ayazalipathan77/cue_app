Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

✓ No data collected

CUE is an offline wireless prompter application for Android. This policy describes how the app handles your information.

Data Collection

CUE collects no personal data. No account is required. Nothing is stored on external servers. All communication happens directly between your devices and never leaves them.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions solely to enable local device-to-device communication via Google Nearby Connections:

  • Bluetooth (Advertise, Connect, Scan) — Used to discover and connect to nearby devices running CUE.
  • Nearby Wi-Fi Devices — Used for high-speed peer-to-peer data transfer between devices.
  • Location — Required by the Android operating system for Bluetooth and Wi-Fi scanning. CUE does not access, record, or transmit your physical location at any time.

Internet Access

The app declares the INTERNET permission as required by the Google Nearby Connections library internals. CUE itself makes no network requests and sends no data over the internet.

Third-Party Services

CUE does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

Children

CUE does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
